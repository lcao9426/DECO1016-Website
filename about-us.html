About Us

As a subsidiary of the Australian Red Cross, Australian Red Cross Lifeblood oversees the collection, management, and distribution of the life-saving donations of blood, plasma, platelets, and other biological products.

Funded by the Australian government, Lifeblood is also an industry leader in the research and development of other clinical services such as biological banking, immunogenetics, diagnostics. During 2018 - 2019, Lifeblood, in collaboration with the National Blood Authority, were able to better understand how blood is used during pregnancy, birth, and in newborn babies, evidence that has been used to improve guidelines regarding blood transfusions.
